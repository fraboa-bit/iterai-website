01 — What we do

Intelligence is not
a product.
It is a journey.

Iter — from Latin, a path. iterAI is built on this idea: AI adoption is not a one-time deployment. It is an iterative process of discovery, implementation, learning, and growth.

We work alongside organisations to embed artificial intelligence where it creates real, lasting value — across research, operations, and decision-making — with an emphasis on sustainability and scientific rigour.
